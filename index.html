About Me

I thrive at the intersection of marketing, consumer healthcare, and technology. Over the past 8+ years, I have built, scaled, and transformed some of India's most recognized FMCG and healthcare brands, driving category expansion, digital innovation, and consumer engagement.

Currently, as Associate Director – Marketing at Cipla Health Ltd, I am leading the evolution of the Smoking/Tobacco Cessation category with Nicotex and Nicogum, driving its ₹1000 Cr growth aspiration by integrating tech-driven marketing strategies and consumer-first innovations.

Previously, at Godrej Consumer Products Ltd (GCPL), I led the ₹500 Cr+ Fabric Care & Hygiene portfolio, launching India's first powder-to-liquid body wash and driving double-digit growth across categories.

I hold an MBA in International Business from the Indian Institute of Foreign Trade, Delhi, and a Master of Science in Pharmacology and Toxicology from NIPER, Mohali. My educational background and diverse professional experiences have equipped me with a unique blend of strategic and creative skills.

I am deeply passionate about leveraging technology to redefine brand experiences, especially in the evolving landscape of D2C, e-commerce, and health-tech.
